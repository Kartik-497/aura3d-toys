✦ FAQ

Questions Decoded

Is this suitable for my 9-year-old? +

Absolutely! Our Beginner kits (like The Ancient Globe) are designed for ages 8+. The instructions are fully visual — like a LEGO manual — making them accessible for younger builders. If they love the challenge of puzzles, they'll thrive. The sense of pride when finished is worth every minute.

Are these kits suitable for girls as well? +

100%! Our kits are designed for all curious minds. Many of our most passionate builders are girls — and parents constantly tell us their daughters are more engaged with our kits than their brothers. Spatial thinking, creative problem-solving, and the pride of building something beautiful — these transcend gender.

Do we need glue or special tools? +

No glue, no tools, nothing extra needed. Our pieces are precision laser-cut to interlock tightly with a satisfying snap. We include a small piece of sandpaper and wood wax in every box for a final polish — that's all you'll ever need.

What if a piece snaps or gets lost? +

Our "Lost a Piece" Guarantee has you covered. Simply email our support team with the kit name and part number visible on the broken/missing piece. We'll mail a brand new replacement piece completely free of charge, no questions asked.

How long does shipping take to India? +

Standard shipping across India takes 5–7 business days. Customers who order our Explorer Duo or Mastermind Vault bundles are automatically upgraded to Priority 3-Day Shipping at no extra cost.
